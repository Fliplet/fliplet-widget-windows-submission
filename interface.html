Step 2 - Submit your app

To submit your app to the Windows Store you will need to login at Windows Store Dashboard with your account details.

Once logged in, if you are starting a new submission you need to click Create a new app.

Now you just need to follow the steps on by one in the Windows Store Dashboard website. If you get lost at any point, Microsoft provides extensive documentation along the way and check our recommendations in our help articles.
